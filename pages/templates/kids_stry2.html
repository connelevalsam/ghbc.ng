TOPIC:

LITTLE SAMUEL

BIBLE TEXT:

1 Samuel 1:28 “So now I give him to the Lord. For his whole life he will be given over to the Lord and he worshiped the Lord there”.

TEACHING AIM:

To teach children to dedicate their lives to serving God.

LESSON

Did you know that many mothers pray for their children before they are born? Yes Samuel’s mother Hannah prayed for Him to be born. When Samuel was born his mother dedicated him to God and he grew up in the temple serving God. Though he was just a little child he dedicated himself to serving God under priest Eli.

Samuel could hear God speak and later became a prophet and a wise judge in Israel, God instructed Samuel to anoint Saul as the first king of Israel and later anointed David little son of Jesse to be king. You can also make a different in your local church by allowing God to use you when you dedicate your life to serving Him.
