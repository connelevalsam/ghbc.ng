TOPIC:

TRUSTING GOD

BIBLE TEXT:

Proverbs 3: 5 – 6 “ Trust in Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding”.

TEACHING AIM:

Teaching children to put their trust in God.

LESSON

Trust is belief, dependence, and assurance, reliance on the character, ability, strength or truth of someone.

The book of Nehemiah tells us a story about trusting God to succeed even when people are against you or your project.

Nehemiah was a government worker not a builder or contractor, but when he saw that the walls of Jerusalem have torn down by God’s enemy, he decided to do something about it.

Israel’s enemies didn’t like the idea and did everything to stop him. But Nehemiah cried to God and put his trust on Him. Nehemiah ignored all the attack and distraction and depends on God. In the end he rebuilds the walls of Jerusalem because he prayed and trusted God.

It is not easy to do something and succeed when the odds are against you. But with God all things are possible.
