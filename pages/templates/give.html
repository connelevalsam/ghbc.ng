Tithes and Offering

The Bible teaches us a baseline standard for giving which includes, tithes, offering, seed faith etc. When we give the first 10% of our income to the church, we put God first in our lives. Giving to God is an act of worship that expresses our gratitude, faith, and love for Him, giving to humanity is an expression of our love for God towards them.

Welfare & Reliefs Packages

Sharing the love of Christ means bringing help and hope to people in need. GHBC does this by partnering with communities, organizations and local churches to bring relief materials to the needy and less priviledge.

Crusades and Missions

At GHBC, we will do anything short of sin to spread God's truth and love around the world. We spread the Gospel via Crusades and Missions outreaches both locally and internationally. Also, by leveraging technology, we are able to bring the gospel to you.
