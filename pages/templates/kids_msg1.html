TOPIC:

THE COMMANDMENT

BIBLE TEXT:

Exodus 20:1 “I am the Lord your God who brought you out of Egypt, out of the land of slavery”.

TEACHING AIM:

To teach children to keep the commandment always.

LESSON

One day the Hebrew people camped in Mount Sinai. God called Moses to come up to the top of the mountain. He gave Moses some important rules to live by so that the Hebrew would know how to please God.

He called these rules the Ten Commandment.
  1. Love God more than you love anything or anyone else
  2. Do not make idols to worship
  3. Use God’s name with love and respect
  4. Remember the Lord’s day and keep it holy
  5. Honour your father and mother
  6. Do not murder
  7. Be faithful to your husband or wife
  8. Do not steal
  9. Do not lie
  10. Do not desire what belong to your neighbour
