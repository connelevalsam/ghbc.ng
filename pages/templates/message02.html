TOPIC:

THE GATES OF HELL SHALL NOT PREVAIL

BIBLE TEXT:

Exodus 1:15-22

Introduction: When Pharaoh discovered that persecution, affliction and all kinds of suffering he brought upon the children of Israel, could not stop their fruitfulness and increase, he decided to use the Hebrew Midwives, so that all male children would be killed. Again, God intervened.

  1. God’s instrument of intervention;

    “And he (pharaoh) said, “When you do the duties of the midwives for the Hebrew women, and see them on the birth stools, if it is a son, then you shall kill him; but if it is a daughter, then she shall live.

    But the midwives feared God, and did not do as the king of Egypt commanded them, but saved the male children. vs 16,17. Pharaoh ordered that only the male children should be killed, why? The Bible says “A man is the image and the glory of God, but the woman is the glory of man” – 1 Cor 11:7. The devil knows that once he captures man, the glory of God is taken. That is why in Genesis when the woman ate the forbidden fruit, nothing happened, but immediately she gave the husband (man) to eat, God came down. Once the head is cut off, the whole body will fall. That is why the bible says in Matt. 2:16 – ‘Then Herod, when he saw that he was deceived by the wise men, was exceedingly angry, and he sent forth and put to death all the male children who were in Bethlehem and in all its districts, from two years old and under, according to the time which he had determined from the wise men”. The “seed of the woman” Gen.3:15 will certainly be a man. The devil is at war with the male folk. The prophet who will bring Israel out of Egypt will certainly be a man, the devil knows that.

    How did God intervene for His people? By putting His fear in the hearts of the midwives; “But the midwives feared God…..”. We should always pray that God will put His fear in the heart of all the people connected to us in one way or the other e.g our doctors, those working with us, like house helps, colleagues, bosses in the office etc. Sometimes He brings His fear, dread and terror upon the people to disarm them. “And they journeyed, and the terror of God was upon the cities that were all around them, and they did not pursue the sons of Jacob”. Gen 35:5

  2. God blessed the midwives;

    “And so it was because the midwives feared God that He provided households for them” v 21.

    God rewards faithfulness, fear of God and obedience. And said, “By myself have I sworn, says the Lord, because you have done this thing and have not withheld your son, your only son – blessing I will bless you, and multiplying I will multiply your descendants as the stars of heaven and as the sand which is on the sea shores and your descendants shall possess the gate of their enemies. In your seed all the nations of the earth shall be blessed, because you have obeyed my voice”. Gen 22:16-18. “God dealt well with the midwives… and “He provided households for them” Ex 1:20,21.

  3. Pharaoh (the devil) never gives up

    – “So Pharaoh commanded all his people; saying, “every son who is born you shall cast into the river, and every daughter you shall save alive” v. 22.
