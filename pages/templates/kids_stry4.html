TOPIC:

JESUS IS BORN

BIBLE TEXT:

Mathematics 1: 2. “And she will bring forth a son and you shall call His name JESUS, for He will save His people from their sins.”

TEACHING AIM:

To teach children about the significant birth of Christ.

LESSON

It was a quiet night; stars were twinkling in the black sky, in the fields outside of Bethlehem. Sheep were setting in for the night while their shepherds watched over them.

A baby is born in Bethlehem His name is Jesus Saviour of the whole world.

The birth of Jesus Christ was prophesied in Isaiah 9: 6-7 He reveals some character of Jesus with names that will reflect His nature. Jesus had to be born as a child, grew up on earth but lived a perfect and sinless life.

He came from Heaven to earth as a son of God to save us from our sins.
