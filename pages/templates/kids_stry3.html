ABRAHAM FOLLOWS GOD

BIBLE TEXT:

Genesis 12:1 The Lord has said to Abraham, leave your country, your people and your fatherâ€™s household and to the land I will show you.

TEACHING AIM:

To teach children that God can move them out of their comfort zone to a place He wants them to be.

LESSON

Abraham obeyed God and left his relatives and country because God told him to go to a new land far away. God promised to give him many children and make him father of nations.

God also changed his name from Abram to Abraham and blessed him and his wife Sarah with a son named Isaac at old age. It pays so much to obey God. Abraham trusted God so much and followed Him where He led.
