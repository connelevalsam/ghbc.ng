What to expect at Great Harvest Bible Church?

At GHBC, you'll be welcomed into a friendly, casual environment by people who are excited to see you. We want you to have an idea of what to expect when you arrive at GHBC. All you have to do from here is come as you are and find out more for yourself.

At a weekly service, you can expect:

  • Each service to last two hours.
  • Thought-provoking and engaging messages based on the Bible.
  • Teaching from Senior Pastor Apostle Elias Emmanue or a GHBC pastor.
  • Soul-out worship music.

Find your personal next step:

  • A place to belong. LifeTeams are the people you can laugh, grow, and serve with. We have teams for men, women, youths, singers, and more!
  • A way to grow. We want to equip you with the tools to develop and explore as a fully devoted follower of Christ. As an individual, family, or part of a LifeTeam, you can visit the Media for resources to keep learning.
  • A chance to make a difference. God created you with a purpose and gave you gifts and skills that you can use to change lives in your area and the world. Serve at GHBC or connect with one of our Missions partners to get started.

Your family will enjoy:

  • A place just for kids. Every Sunday, kids and newborns (including primary school pupils), learn the truth about who God is and who they are because of Him through basic foundational lessons, small groups, and trusted adult leadership.
  • An Evening just for teens. Teens meets twice a month on chosen Saturdays. It is for those between 13 - 19 years. Loaded with fast-paced worship and interactive activities, it addresses real life issues teens face each day.

Your whole family is invited.

We are one church meeting in one location. Moms, dads, and children of every age can find something to love about Great Harvest Bible Church!
