TOPIC:

THE DREAMER

BIBLE TEXT:

Genesis 37: 5 “Joseph had a dream and when he told it to his brothers they hated him all the more”

TEACHING AIM:

To teach children God can speak to people through dreams.

LESSON

Dream is a series of thoughts, vision, or feelings that happen during sleep.

God sometimes speaks to people through dream. Most dreams are message from God and some are not. God gave dreams to Joseph and also wisdom to interpret it, He also gave dream to the cupbearer and the baker while in prison.

God had given dream to many children before as a message to their parents, church or even their friends.

When you are close to God and listen to Him He can speak to you through dream also.
